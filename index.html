Riddle

"I’m everywhere but can’t be seen."

"I can be in pieces but make things whole."

"I have a name but I’m not alive."

"I can break but can’t heal."

"I exist in silence but can bring noise."

"I follow instructions but never think."

"I travel in bits but I'm not a letter."

"Without me, systems are incomplete. What am I?"
